Hello, I’m Eley! I am a PhD student studying robotics & AI in the Stanford ARMLab, where I am advised by Prof. Monroe Kennedy III. My current research involves developing cooperative robotic agents for human-robot joint action tasks, and evaluating how well they work with humans. I was supported by the NSF Graduate Research Fellowship.

Previously, I was fortunate to have been advised by Prof. Nanshu Lu on studying the mechanics of stretchable electronics. I received my B.S. in Mechanical Engineering at The University of Texas at Austin, and M.S. in Mechanical Engineering at Stanford University.
